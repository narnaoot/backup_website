Two Kinds of Problems.

in practice
🩹
Duct Tape
When something doesn't hold together — I build the structure
Siloed teams Inconsistent data Unclear ownership No shared process Missing documentation Scattered reporting

The pieces are real. The seams aren't. Three teams running parallel and not talking, five spreadsheets answering one question five ways. I build the seams: shared definitions, operating rhythm, one map everyone agrees on.

🪣
Drano
When something isn't moving — I clear the path
Stuck workflows Manual bottlenecks Stalled projects Buried insights Waiting on sign-off Technical debt blocking features

The work is real. The team is ready. Something's just stuck — an owner unclear, a queue unprioritized, a decision waiting on someone who doesn't know they're the bottleneck. I find what it is and clear it.
